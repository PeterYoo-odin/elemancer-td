## 5. Suggested tuning diff (for review — NOT auto-applied)

### 🔻 NERF — Chain Reactor relic (comboRamp)
- `src/sim/drafts.ts`: combo card comboRamp multiplier **1.6 → 1.45**
- On scarce early-game resources, Chain-Reactor-greedy Tempest+Blizzard survives 6.0 waves vs 2.0 for the balanced control (3.02× deeper) and saturates the 6× combo cap 0.0 waves earlier (25% of runs vs 25%). comboRamp is the escalator; a small trim slows the snowball without gutting the relic.

### 🔻 NERF — Blizzard branch (frost)
- `src/game/towers.ts`: blizzard branch range **4.0 → 3.5**
- Blizzard’s board-wide slow keeps the pack clustered so Tempest chains hit everything and the combo ramps. Narrowing the aura preserves its "wide chill" identity while cutting the free clustering that feeds the degenerate loop.

### 🔺 BUFF — Prism branch (arcane)
- `src/game/towers.ts`: prism branch damage **52 → 66**
- Prism sits at the bottom of the tower sweep: it neither buffs as widely as Amplify nor out-damages a real DPS branch. A modest damage bump makes the "buffs AND blasts" fantasy competitive.

_Discipline: every suggestion keeps the "no immunities, rates are fair" rule — it tunes magnitudes, never adds hard counters._
